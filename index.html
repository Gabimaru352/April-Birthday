a poem for you
In the quiet heights where the mist hangs low,
where the Chin Hills wake in a pale pink glow,
the cherry blossoms begin their grace,
but they find their rival in your face.
They call them Pawi — the pride of the peak,
with a gentle strength and a bloom so unique.
But even the forests of Falam in bloom
cannot light the corners of a quiet room
the way your smile — like a sudden spring —
can change the tune that the mountains sing.
Your name is a promise, April, a breath of air,
a Flower (Paw) that's delicate, vibrant, and rare.
While the petals may fall when the season is through,
the light in my world is eternally you.
So here's to the girl with the mountain soul,
who makes every broken heart feel whole.
Happy Birthday, my favourite sight —
my April Paw, my blossom of light.

"One day I will call your name
from the Chin Hills —
where the Pawi trees are already waiting for you."
